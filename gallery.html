Portrait Power

Cheetahs have distinctive black "tear marks" that reduce glare from the sun, just like football players' eye black!

Speed Machine

When running at full speed, cheetahs spend more time in the air than on the ground - they literally fly!

Cute Cubs

Baby cheetahs are born with a fluffy gray mane that helps them blend into tall grass and avoid predators!

Rest Time

After a high-speed chase, cheetahs need 20-30 minutes to recover before they can hunt again. Speed comes at a cost!

Family Life

Unlike other big cats, cheetah mothers raise their cubs alone for up to 18 months, teaching them essential hunting skills!

Spot Pattern

Each cheetah has a unique spot pattern, like fingerprints! No two cheetahs have identical markings.

About This Gallery

These beautiful cheetah photos showcase the incredible diversity and beauty of these amazing cats. Each image tells a story about their behavior, habitat, and unique characteristics.
