Meet the Speed Demon of Africa

Cheetahs are the fastest land animals on Earth, capable of reaching incredible speeds!

Amazing Cheetah Facts

  • 🏃‍♂️ Can reach 120 km/h (75 mph) in just 3 seconds
  • 👀 Have distinctive black "tear marks" from eyes to mouth
  • 🐾 Cannot roar - they chirp, purr, and meow instead
  • ⚡ Can only maintain top speed for 20-30 seconds
  • 🦴 Have a flexible spine that acts like a spring
  • 👁️ Have excellent eyesight and can spot prey from 5km away
